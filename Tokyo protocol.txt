1.	Current candle definition (applies everywhere) Current candle = the latest bar on the chart at the moment of decision (the last candle shown on the image). All indicator readings use the last 3 fully completed bars before the current candle; Direction uses the current candle’s price vs Pivot P.

3.	Indicator State Buckets (must match journal examples) All indicator readings must match the journal state labels, not generic TA, and are always taken from the last 3 completed bars before the current candle. A. MACD Lines (last 3 completed bars)
-UP_SEPARATED — lines clearly apart, both angled up -DOWN_SEPARATED — lines clearly apart, both angled down -FLAT_NEAR_ZERO / FLAT_HUGGING_ZERO — lines close together around zero with no slope -TURNING / MIXED — slope shift from up→flat/down or down→flat/up -CROSSING / DOWN_CROSSING / UP_CROSSING — lines crossing each other or crossing zero B. MACD Histogram (last 3 completed bars)
-STRONG_POSITIVE — positive and increasing -SMALL_POSITIVE — positive but small; shallow build -STRONG_NEGATIVE — negative and increasingly negative -SMALL_NEGATIVE — negative but small -SHRINKING_TO_ZERO / POS_SHRINK / NEG_SHRINK — bars contracting toward zero C. OBV Slope (last 3 completed bars)
-UP — clear upward slope -DOWN — clear downward slope -UP_SHALLOW — upward but gentle -DOWN_SHALLOW — downward but gentle -FLAT / FLAT_HEAVY — sideways, minimal net change D. OBV Divergence
-NONE — no HL/LH mismatch vs price -BULLISH — price downtrend but OBV rising -BEARISH — price uptrend but OBV falling E. RSI (14-period, last 3 completed bars)
-OVERBOUGHT — RSI > 70 -OVERSOLD — RSI < 30 -NEUTRAL — RSI between 30 and 70 -STRONG_BULL — RSI > 60 (sustained above in uptrend) -STRONG_BEAR — RSI < 40 (sustained below in downtrend) F. RSI Divergence
-NONE — no HL/LH mismatch vs price -BULLISH — price making lower lows but RSI making higher lows -BEARISH — price making higher highs but RSI making lower highs 3. Conviction (mechanical, no subjective input) Conviction = indicator alignment score relative to Direction (now including RSI as a fourth component). A. LONG conviction rules (Direction = LONG): HIGH conviction LONG All conditions must be true: MACD = UP_SEPARATED histogram = STRONG_POSITIVE (strong positive build) OBV slope = UP (not shallow) OBV divergence = NONE RSI = STRONG_BULL or NEUTRAL (above 50) and RSI divergence = NONE MEDIUM conviction LONG:
Exactly three of the bias components are aligned (or two if RSI is NEUTRAL but not overbought): MACD = UP_SEPARATED histogram = SMALL_POSITIVE or slightly positive OBV slope = UP_SHALLOW or FLAT OBV divergence = NONE RSI = NEUTRAL (above 50) and RSI divergence = NONE LOW conviction LONG:
